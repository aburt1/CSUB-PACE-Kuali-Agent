## Reference Intent

Select one:

- Design reference: borrow quality, clarity, layout, tone, and organization.
- Pattern reference: borrow workflow, notification, permission, routing, reporting, or review patterns where they fit.
- Functional copy: copy concrete fields, workflow steps, emails, assignees, integrations, or permissions only when explicitly confirmed.

## Testing Checklist

- Target still matches its own business process.
- No reference-specific fields were copied without confirmation.
- No reference-specific approvers, groups, users, or permissions were copied without confirmation.
- No reference-specific email promises were copied without confirmation.
- Workflow routes correctly for each target outcome.
- Send-back, denial, approval, and completion paths work.
- Notifications match the target process.
- CSUB.edu source-derived language is cited and confirmed where policy-sensitive.
